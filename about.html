Who We Are

A Story Rooted in Nature

Luxury Hill Resorts was born from a simple belief — that the finest holidays are those spent surrounded by nature, without sacrificing an ounce of comfort or luxury. We started with a single property in the misty hills of Ooty, and over the years, grew into a collection of four exceptional retreats spanning Tamil Nadu and Kerala.

Our resorts cater to families seeking memorable getaways, couples celebrating love, solo travellers finding themselves in silence, and groups of friends creating stories worth telling. Every property reflects our commitment to sustainability, local culture and heartfelt service.

We don't just offer a place to sleep — we offer a feeling. The feeling of waking up to mountains in the morning. The warmth of a campfire under the stars. The flavour of a meal made from produce grown just metres from your plate. That is Luxury Hill Resorts.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Three pillars that guide everything we do — from building our resorts to training our staff.

Nature First

We design our resorts to live in harmony with the environment — using sustainable materials, minimising our footprint and protecting the ecosystems we call home.

Heartfelt Hospitality

Every guest is treated like a cherished friend. From the warm welcome to the tearful farewell, our team is dedicated to making your stay truly special and personal.

Curated Luxury

Luxury, for us, means the right things in the right places — premium linens, exceptional cuisine, thoughtful amenities and spaces that feel both grand and deeply comforting.

Family & Couples

Our resorts are crafted for every kind of traveller — families making memories, couples on romantic retreats, solo explorers and friends celebrating milestones together.

Hill Station Experts

With properties across Ooty, Kodaikanal, Yercaud and Cochin, we have deep-rooted expertise in South India's most beautiful destinations and what makes them unique.

Excellence Always

We never settle. Every stay is an opportunity to raise the bar — in service, in experience, in the little details that turn a good holiday into an unforgettable one.
