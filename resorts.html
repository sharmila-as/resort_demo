Nilgiri Hills, Tamil Nadu · 2240m

Ooty Resort

The Queen of Hill Stations welcomes you with open arms. Our Ooty resort sits nestled among rolling tea plantations and silver oak forests, offering a retreat that feels worlds away from the city. The cool mountain air, the scent of eucalyptus, and the panoramic views of the Nilgiri valleys create an atmosphere of total serenity.

Perfect for families, honeymooners and those seeking a peaceful sojourn in nature's lap.

Luxury Cottages Valley Views Campfire Restaurant Free WiFi Tea Garden Walk Parking Wellness Centre
Palani Hills, Tamil Nadu · 2133m

Kodaikanal Resort

The Princess of Hill Stations is where romance meets nature. Our Kodaikanal resort perches above the famous star-shaped lake, surrounded by pine forests and shola grasslands. The misty mornings, cool evenings and spectacular sunsets over the Palani Hills are experiences that guests return for, year after year.

An ideal destination for couples, nature photographers and those who seek quiet introspection.

Lake-View Suites Misty Views Bonfire Nights Multi-Cuisine Free WiFi Cycling Trails Parking Photography Tours
Shevaroy Hills, Tamil Nadu · 1515m

Yercaud Resort

The Jewel of the South is a hidden paradise tucked away in the Shevaroy Hills. Our Yercaud resort sits amidst coffee plantations, orange groves and forests teeming with migratory birds. Less touristy than its famous neighbours, Yercaud offers a rare sense of genuine solitude and natural abundance.

Perfect for families, birdwatchers and those seeking a truly off-the-beaten-path retreat.

Forest Cottages Coffee Estate Campfire Organic Cuisine Free WiFi Bird Watching Parking Stargazing
Kerala, India · Coastal

Cochin Resort

The Queen of the Arabian Sea offers a different kind of luxury — one steeped in centuries of history, spice trade heritage and the gentle rhythm of the backwaters. Our Cochin resort blends colonial-era architecture with contemporary comfort, placing you at the heart of Kerala's most culturally rich destination.

A perfect blend of culture, history, backwater cruises and sun-soaked Kerala cuisine.

Heritage Suites Backwater View Houseboat Tours Kerala Cuisine Free WiFi Beach Access Parking Ayurvedic Spa
